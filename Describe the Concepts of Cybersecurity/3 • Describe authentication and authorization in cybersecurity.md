<p>What are the three types of authentication?<br>
<code>Something you know, something you have, and something you are.</code></p>

<p>What is credential stuffing?<br>
<code>You use the same username and password at many sites. There's a security breach at one site, and attackers use your compromised usernames and passwords at many other sites.</code></p>

<p>What are the three principles of Zero Trust?<br>
<code>Verify explicitly, assume breach, and use least privileged access.</code></p>
