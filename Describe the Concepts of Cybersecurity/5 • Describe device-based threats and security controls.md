<p>In the context of cybersecurity, what can be considered a device?<br>
<code>Anything you can touch but also connect to something else</code></p>

<p>What is device hardening?<br>
<code>Minimizing device vulnerabilities</code></p>

<p>What is jailbreaking?<br>
<code>When a user finds unofficial methods to get full access to the core systems of the device.</code></p>
