<h2>Prepared the Microsoft Defender XDR workspace</h2>





<p>At the Microsoft Defender portal Home screen, I scrolled down the navigation menu items to the Assets section, and selected Devices.</p>

<img width="979" height="497" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d4d8ffdf-f94e-438d-b43c-8fffb5c0e2c3" />



<p>The process to deploy the Defender XDR workspace started and I saw messages saying loading and Initializing displayed at the top of the page, and then I saw an image of a coffee mug and a message that reads: Hang on! We're preparing new spaces for your data and connecting them.<br>It took approximately 5 minutes to finish.</p>

<img width="1030" height="577" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5a3a2d84-e3e0-4310-8373-da31fa5087d0" />

<p>When the new workspace initialization completes successfully, the Home portal page will display a Get your SIEM and XDR in one place banner. And, in Settings, the Microsoft Defender XDR General settings for Account, Email notifications, Preview Features, Alert service settings, Permissions and roles and Streaming API are now turned on.</p>
